We are affiliated to Government's Moon-Mission 2048 under which we are registering interested ones to seek Citizenship card to live on moon. It is expected that on March 2048, a team of curious people from our country will be sent to moon to find out the challenges that we could face while living on a different place than earth. They will be sent for 4 months to experience and analyze the surviving conditions there. This project is really important as our Mars-Mission 2060 totally depends on its success.
